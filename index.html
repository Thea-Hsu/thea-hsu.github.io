Research Interests

My research interest is to apply Interpretable Machine Learning to interdisciplinary fields.
In particular, I am interested in:

  • Interpretable ML: Concept Bottleneck Models, Rule-based Reasoning, Attribution-based Explanations.
  • Large Language Models: Interpretability, Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Reasoning, Multimodal LLMs.
  • AI for Science: Healthcare, Environmental Science, Material Science.
